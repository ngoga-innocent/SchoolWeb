Our School Values

Respect

Teaching students to treat others with respect, regardless of differences in background, beliefs, or opinions. This includes respecting oneself, peers, teachers, and the broader community.

Honesty and Integrity

Encouraging honesty and integrity in all aspects of life, including academic work, relationships, and personal conduct. Emphasizing the importance of being truthful and trustworthy.

Responsibility

Fostering a sense of responsibility towards oneself and others. Teaching students to take ownership of their actions, make responsible decisions, and fulfill their obligations and commitments.

Compassion and Empathy:

Cultivating compassion and empathy towards others, promoting kindness, understanding, and consideration for the feelings and experiences of others.

Fairness and Justice

Instilling a sense of fairness and justice, teaching students to treat others fairly and advocate for equality and justice in their communities.

Courage and Perseverance

Encouraging courage and perseverance in the face of challenges and adversity. Teaching students to overcome obstacles, take risks, and persist in pursuit of their goals.
